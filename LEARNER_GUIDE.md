# The Launchpad — Learner Guide

---

## How It Works

The program runs over **8 weeks**. Each week unlocks once you complete the previous one.

Every week has:

| Activity | What to do |
|---|---|
| **Quiz** | Answer questions on the week's topics. You need to pass (score above the threshold) to move on. You get one attempt — make it count. |
| **Project** | Submit a real deliverable. Your mentor reviews and grades it. |
| **Reflection** | Write what you learned. AI evaluates it and gives feedback. Revise if needed. |

Complete all three to unlock the next week.

---

## XP & Levels

Every action earns you **XP (experience points)**. XP determines your level.

**How you earn XP:**

| Action | XP |
|---|---|
| Pass a quiz (first attempt) | 100 XP |
| Pass a quiz with 90%+ | +20 XP bonus |
| Pass a quiz with 95%+ | +50 XP bonus |
| Pass a quiz on retry | 70 XP |
| Project graded | 200 XP |
| Project graded with 90%+ | +50 XP bonus |
| Complete a full week | 150 XP |
| Active week (streak bonus) | 50 XP |

**Levels:**

| Level | Title | XP Required |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Rookie | 0 |
| 2 | Builder | 1,000 |
| 3 | Operator | 3,000 |
| 4 | Growth Hacker | 6,000 |
| 5 | Product Ninja | 10,000 |
| 6 | Founding Team | 15,000 |
| 7 | Chief Architect | 20,000 |

Keep a weekly streak and your title gets a prefix — *Focused Builder*, *Relentless Operator*, etc.

---

## Badges

Badges are earned for specific achievements. Some are one-time, some repeat every week.

**Weekly badges** (earn them fresh each week):

| Badge | How to earn |
|---|---|
| 💎 Perfectionist | Score 90%+ on every quiz in the week — first attempt only |
| 📦 Ship It | Get all your projects graded that week |

**Program badges** (earned once):

| Badge | How to earn |
|---|---|
| 🏅 Week 1–8 badges | Complete each week fully |
| 🧠 Quiz Master | Score 95%+ on 5 or more quizzes across the program |
| 🔥 Streak Lord | Stay active for 4 consecutive weeks |
| ⚡ Early Operator | Founding cohort member — you already have this |
| ⚡ First 1K | Reach 1,000 XP |
| 💫 5K Club | Reach 5,000 XP |
| 🏆 10K Club | Reach 10,000 XP |

---

## Quick Tips

- **Quizzes matter** — first-attempt scores unlock bonus XP and Perfectionist. Don't rush.
- **Submit projects on time** — you need the grade to earn Ship It for that week.
- **Write real reflections** — the AI checks for depth, not length. Be specific.
- **Stay consistent** — one active week a week keeps your streak alive and adds 50 XP each time.
- **Check the bell** — your notification bell flags pending tasks so nothing slips.
